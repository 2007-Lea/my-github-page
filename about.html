About Us

Dipheko Motors was founded with a simple but powerful vision — to bring quality, reliable vehicles to the people of Botswana while delivering service built on honesty and respect. Born and raised in Botswana, the founder of Dipheko Motors saw the need for a dealership that truly understands local drivers — our roads, our economy, and our lifestyle. What started as a passion for cars and entrepreneurship grew into a mission: to create a dealership where customers feel valued, not pressured. At Dipheko Motors, we understand that buying a car is not just a purchase — it’s an investment in your future, your family, and your mobility. That’s why every vehicle in our inventory is carefully selected and inspected to ensure it meets our standards of performance, durability and value. We proudly serve customers across Gaborone and surrounding areas, helping young professionals, families, and business owners find vehicles that match their ambitions and budgets.

Our commitment is simple:

✔️ Transparent and honest pricing

✔️ Quality vehicles you can trust

✔️ A hassle-free buying experience

✔️ Professional and friendly service

✔️ Long-term customer relationships

Dipheko Motors is more than a dealership — it’s a growing local brand built on integrity, ambition, and excellence.

As Botswana continues to grow, we are proud to grow with it — one satisfied customer at a time.

Dipheko Motors. Driven by Trust. Built for Botswana..
